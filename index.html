LELC — Latent Emotion–Logic Coupling

Latent Emotion–Logic Coupling (LELC) is a universal axis observable in large language‑model embeddings that reflects how emotional valence influences logical reasoning. Using paired emotional‑neutral prompts and dimensionality reduction of hidden‑state vectors, a consistent direction — the LELC axis — emerges, linking affective framing to reasoning coherence. This framework bridges cognitive neuroscience, emotional computation, and AI alignment, offering insights into how affective tone modulates machine logic.
